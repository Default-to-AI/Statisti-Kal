How should we integrate P-Value Calculation?

Inside the Hypothesis Testing tab

A

Mode Switch (Toggle)

A dynamic toggle at the top of the tab. Changes the form fields based on what you want to calculate.

Calculate P-Value
Form fields for Hypothesis Test
B

Unified Results (Calculate Both)

No toggle needed. You enter your test statistic/data, and the results section automatically shows both the decision and the exact p-value.
